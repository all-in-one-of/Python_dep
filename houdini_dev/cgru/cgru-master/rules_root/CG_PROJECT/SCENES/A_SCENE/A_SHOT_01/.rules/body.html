Shot asset stands for:

  • Search for sources, for preview them, open in explorer and copy to Nuke.
  • Show result sequences and make dailies.
  • Upload files (dailies) for remote artists.
  • Comment shot.
  • Explode dailies to draw annotations.

Shot thumbnail is updated automatically every time Shot asset opened but not faster than a special period of time.

Shot body can be used to display shot specific information and tasks.
